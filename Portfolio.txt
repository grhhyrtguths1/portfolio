Missile Command
Retro, Arcade Game

A recreation of the 1980s game Missile Command
You must protect your city from a barrage of missiles in order to advance to reach the highest score!
This main mechanic of this game is to intercept as many missiles as possible with the mouse and arrow keys, be resourceful with your ammo!
